I.T.I. Majorana

The I.T.I. E. Majorana has been operating since 1976 at its Grugliasco location training industrial experts in computer science,

electronics and in telecommunications, sectors in which significant opportunities are observed for young people both to look for a first occupation and for the continuation of their studies.

Graduation

The full cycle lasts five years and allows for the attainment of the Technical Education Diploma with which one can immediately enter the world of work or continue studies in all faculties University and at the Polytechnic.

At the end of the fourth class (for a duration of 4-5 weeks) and during the fifth class, the following is offered students the opportunity to take advantage of an internship period in companies in the electronics, computer and telecommunications industries, in order to more consciously orient their future choices. and telecommunications, in order to more consciously orient their future choices.

Educational offerings

Ettore Majorana Industrial Technical Institute trains industrial technical experts in the fields of "Computer Science and Telecommunications," "Electronics and Electrical Engineering," and "Chemistry, Materials and Biotechnology," preparing them to enter quickly in technologically advanced production realities or to successfully pursue university studies. The addresses of study, updated by the high school reform, operate in the field of new technologies and are expanded by courses and activities that further specialize students in emerging issues.
