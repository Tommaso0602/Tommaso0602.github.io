Electronics

The address in Electronics and Electrical Engineering is for students who wish to acquire skills in electrical systems, automation systems and electrical systems. The address includes two articulations, "Electronics" and "Automation and Robotics."

Electronic Articulation

The expert intervenes in the processes of production and conversion of electrical energy, including with alternative energy sources, to optimize consumption and adapt systems and devices to safety standards. He has skills in the design, construction and testing of electrical systems, analog and digital electronic systems, processing and transmission of electrical signals. He is able to program controllers and microprocessors. He finds employment in companies producing and maintaining electronic data acquisition and transmission systems, power generation systems, electrical systems, and construction of electrical and electronic equipment (biomedical equipment, burglar alarms, home appliances, etc...).

Robotics

The expert works in all civil and industrial sectors, with applications in home automation, mechatronics, robotics and industrial automation. He has skills in the design, construction and testing of automatic control systems, development and use of data acquisition systems, programming of controllers and microprocessors. The course aims to foster learning of robotic electronic systems, robot design, simulation and construction techniques. It is required in companies in various industries (food, mechanical, transportation, etc...) that use automated systems whose maintenance and repair it also performs.
