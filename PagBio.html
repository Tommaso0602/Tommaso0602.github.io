Biotechnology

The Chemistry, Materials and Biotechnology course is designed for students interested in science subjects, technologies and their evolution in chemistry and biotechnology.

Sanitary

The expert in Chemistry, Materials and Health Biotechnology has specific skills in the chemical, biological, health and pharmaceutical fields. He/she has skills related to the use of major health technologies in the biomedical, pharmaceutical and food fields and contributes to the promotion of personal and collective health.
